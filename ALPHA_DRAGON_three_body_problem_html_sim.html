Goal: tune the system until all three bodies stay bounded without obvious ejection.
This is an experimental sandbox. “Stable” here means bounded, low-drift, non-ejecting behavior over the observed run window.

What to watch

Stable-ish: bodies remain bounded, no body escapes far outward, and center-of-mass drift stays low.

Unstable: close encounters spike, one body gets slingshotted, trails stretch outward, or readouts get noisy.

Resonance: watch for repeatable timing relationships in how the bodies loop around the shared center.
